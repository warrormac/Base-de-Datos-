	Killer Instinct



The iconic fighting game franchise is back with 
upgraded visuals, over-the-top action, a wild 
cast of combatants, rocking reactive music, and 
C-C-C-Combo Breakers. Choose from a diverse set of 
characters, each with fluid animations, unique 
combat tactics, and flashy special attacks. Utilize 
an open-ended combo system to rack up massive combos 
as your opponent looks for openings to break your 
flow and turn the tides of battle. Go online and 
test yourself against players from all over the world, 
or bone up on your combat skills in detailed tutorials.


Release: Out Now


Developer: Double Helix Games


ESRB: Teen
