	Assassins Creed Origins


Set in mysterious Ancient Egypt, Assassin’s Creed Origins 
is a new beginning. Experience a new way to fight while 
exploring the Great Pyramids and hidden tombs across the 
country of Ancient Egypt, and encounter many memorable 
storylines along your journey. And discover the origin story 
of the Assassin's Brotherhood.


Release: Out Now

Developer: Ubisoft

ESRB: Mature
