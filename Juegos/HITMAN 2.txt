	HITMAN 2


Travel the globe and track your targets 
across exotic sandbox locations in Hitman 2. 
From sun-drenched streets to dark and 
dangerous rainforests, nowhere is safe from 
the world's most deadly assassin, Agent 47. 
Prepare to experience the ultimate spy thriller 
story -- your mission is to eliminate the 
elusive Shadow Client and unravel his militia, 
but upon learning your target's true identity 
and the truth about 47's past, nothing will ever 
be the same.
