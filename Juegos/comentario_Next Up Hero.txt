	Next Up Hero



Next Up Hero is a dungeon crawler featuring 
hand-drawn heroes that hack, slash, bongo-drum, 
boomerang, and jet their way through Ventures. 
Choose your Hero and start rolling dungeons, 
gearing out, and maxing your stats. Invite a 
friend for co-op -- you’re going to need all 
the help you can get.
